About Me

I am a playwright, screenwriter, and theatre director currently based in Toronto. My work for the stage has been presented at theatres and festivals across Canada, most recently Robert (Lark & Whimsy Theatre Collective, shortlisted for the Playwrights Guild Comedy Award and winner of the Cayle Chernin Award), and The Concessions (Touchstone Theatre, Vancouver), which earned a Jessie Award nomination for Outstanding Original Script. My one-act plays Cassandra and Almost, Again can be found in anthologies by Signature Editions.

During my MFA at the University of British Columbia, I received the Jacob Zilber Award for exceptional promise in screenwriting, and my first feature script received an Honourable Mention in the Vancouver International Women in Film Festival’s annual Screenwriting Competition. The script continues to be in development with Trafalgar Street Productions (UK).

I have a passion for working with young writers and theatre makers, and teach workshops and classes regularly at Young People’s Theatre, Centauri Arts Academy, as well as adjudicating for the National Theatre School Festival.
