“Throughout the process of our work together, I was impressed with Briana’s ability to weave subtle human emotions into a narrative with universal appeal. She maintained the truth of her characters in extreme situations while injecting moments of lightness into serious situations. She never veered into sentimentality, and provided actors with meaty roles to dig into. Somehow, she has an instinct for humanity.” 

— Kathleen Flaherty, Playwrights Theatre Centre

“I count Briana Brown among my closest collaborators; with any project, she is able to incisively seek out and find the heart of the material, bringing it to life in surprising and unexpected ways. She is, quietly and modestly, one of the most crucial artists in my sphere.”

— Rob Kempson

“Briana brought out the best in me and the best out of the play I was writing. Her insights into both the actor’s process and the text were invaluable to my writing process, and her intelligent, compassionate leadership inspired all of us – playwright, actors, designers alike – to dig deeper into our work and the story. Her directing style is precise, engaging and serves the play above all.”

— Erin Vandenburg, playwright of Salt
